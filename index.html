Privacy Policy for DirectServe DirectServe respects your privacy. This app is designed to work entirely on your device and your local network. 📌 Information We Collect DirectServe does not collect, store, or share any personal data. We do not collect: Personal information (name, email, phone number) Device identifiers Usage analytics Location data 📡 How the App Works DirectServe creates a local sharing session on your device. Files are shared only over your WiFi or hotspot Other devices can access your files using a local link or QR code All communication happens within your local network 🔒 Data Storage All files remain on your device DirectServe does not upload files to any server No data is stored externally 🔐 Security DirectServe includes optional security features: You can enable a 4-digit PIN from Settings to restrict access You can view and block connected devices 📂 Permissions Used DirectServe uses only the permissions required for core functionality: Network Access To create a local sharing server To allow devices on your network to connect File/Media Access To let you select and share files from your device Foreground Service & Notifications To keep sharing active while the app is running To show session status and allow quick control 🔄 Data Sharing DirectServe does not share any data with third parties Files are only shared with devices you allow on your local network 🌐 Third-Party Services DirectServe does not use: Analytics tools Advertising SDKs External cloud services 👶 Children’s Privacy DirectServe does not knowingly collect any data from children. 🔄 Changes to This Policy This privacy policy may be updated in future versions.
