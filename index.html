Privacy Policy for DirectServe

DirectServe respects your privacy. This app works entirely on your device and your local network.

Information We Collect

DirectServe does not collect, store, or share any personal data.

How the App Works

DirectServe creates a local sharing session on your device. Files are shared only over your WiFi or hotspot.

Data Storage

All files remain on your device. No data is uploaded to any server.

Security

You can enable an optional 4-digit PIN from Settings and block connected devices.

Permissions

  • Network access to create local sharing session
  • File access to select and share files
  • Foreground service for active sharing session

Data Sharing

No data is shared with third parties.

Third-Party Services

No analytics, ads, or external services are used.
