Privacy Policy for DirectServe

DirectServe respects your privacy. This app works entirely on your device and your local network.

Information We Collect

DirectServe does not collect, store, or share any personal data.

How the App Works

DirectServe creates a local sharing session on your device. Files, media content, and live screen feeds are shared exclusively over your local Wi-Fi or hotspot network.

Data Storage

All files and temporary transmission logs remain stored on your device. No user data gets uploaded to any cloud platform.

Security

You can secure live access channels by enforcing mandatory multi-digit verification PIN tokens within standard profile layers seamlessly.

Permissions

  • Network access to create local sharing session
  • File access to select and share files
  • Foreground service for active sharing session
  • Notification privileges mapping deployment variables
  • Screen capture allowances supporting media projections

Data Sharing

No data is shared with third parties.

Third-Party Services

No analytics, ads, or external services are used.
